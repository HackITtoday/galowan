Friends & Volunteers of Golowan

Golowan is a huge attraction, not just to people across the UK but also from Europe & the USA. It’s fantastic for both business and the community, boosting local economy by over 1m.

But it costs. In fact every year over £80,000 in charges are accrued and met by PZ Town Council, grants, sponsors, local businesss & individual donations.

You too can help. Show your support by becoming a Friend of Golowan or by signing up as a volunteer. By doing so you'll be helping to keep this great Festival going.

Become a Friend from as little £20 per year & receive a big TY both in our programme & here on the website (see our Contact Us details at the bottom of the page). To become a volunteer simply sign up below!
