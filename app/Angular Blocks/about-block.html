About

With impressive views of the Tour Eiffel and perfectly placed in the centre of the haute couture district of Paris, the Plaza Athenee stands proud on Avenue Montaigne. It’s the ideal place to put down those bags after spending a day perusing the designer boutiques or exploring the city

Sleep

154 indulgent rooms along with 54 suites all with thoughtful decor, lavish furnishings, rich fabrics and textures, many with views of the Tour Eiffel, await guests. There’s also a balanced mixture of tastefully integrated technology

Eat

Eat and drink in true style at the Alain Ducasse restaurant overseen by the Michelin star chef. Alternatively indulge in a lighter bite in the Art Deco Le Relais Plaza Parisian brasserie which regularly hosts jazz nights or a pre-dinner cocktail in the bar

Meet

Choose from 5 highly impressive meetings and events spaces that can cater for up to 200 guests at any one time. An array of flexible layouts are on offer along with state of the art tech. equipment, private dining and expert organisers on site

Play

Exploring the designer boutique shops that surround the Plaza Athenee including Dior, Vuitton, Prada and many more is a must. The chestnut trees that line the Champs Elysees also beckon and Rue Faubourg Saint-Honoré is within easy reach
